== Small Game ==

Idea:
- wave-survival battler
- 1 character which has different ability slots. basically like diablo 3 
    - left click
    - right click
    - space (movement ability)
    - Q is too hard to click
    - N passives
- abilities are *classed/colored* so you can get set bonuses (like guns in brotato)
- elemental themes

Combat:
- don't fill the screen with stuff. Come up with meaningful enemy archetypes and
manually design waves with interesting combinations (like jonathan blow said)
- maybe have a "next wave" indicator so you can pick what to equip
- Like HoT, have toggle for auto aim / auto attack

Enemy Types
- Basic (walks in straight line)
- Shielded
    - shields always take N hits to break, regardless of damage
- Charger
- Splitter
- Sniper

Art stuff:
- For enemy movement animations, just do the squish/squash that brotato
and 9 kings do


Combat "ingredients"
- Damage
    - AOE
    - Flat Damage
    - Attack Speed
